Frequently Bought Together Products

Increase your sales by displaying the products you want to sell frequently together with other products. Display any number of Frequently Bought Products with a product on your website while purchasing. Allow the customer to select the products of their choice while adding to cart.

Salient Features

  • Display the list of the products to the customers that are can be Frequently Bought Together with some product.
  • Add any number of products in the Frequently Bought Together list.
  • If the number of products is more, the products will be displayed in a slider.
  • Allow the customers to select the products of their choice while adding to cart.
  • Price will be changed dynamically in the view while selecting the products.
  • Customizable texts.
