Community Enterprise

Features

  • This module allow user to change multiple products tax at a time.
  • Change Customer tax/Vendor tax or both tax at a time.
  • We can provide multiple way to change product taxes.

Configuration

  • For configuration only have to install the change multiple product tax app.

Change product tax product category wise

  • Select the Product Category wise option to change the product tax category wise.
  • In this option all product taxes are change which product category is matches with entered category.

Change product tax POS category wise

  • In this option product tax will change by its POS category.

Change selected product tax

  • In this option selected product tax will change.

Change product Customer tax

  • In this option only product's customer tax will change.

Change product Vendor tax

  • In this option only product's vendor tax will change.

Change both tax of product

  • In this option product's customer tax and vendor tax both will change.
